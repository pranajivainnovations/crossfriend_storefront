# CrossFriend Storefront — Phased Improvement Roadmap

> Benchmarked against: **IGP.com**, **FNP.com**, **PartyCity.com**, **PartyDelights.co.uk**
> Date: May 2026

---

## Current State Summary

### What CrossFriend Already Has ✅
- Hero section with CTA + trust badges (same-day delivery, premium brands)
- Occasion-based navigation & shopping (Birthday, Anniversary, Festival, Kids, Special)
- Category pages with subcategory drill-down
- Product type filtering (cake, decor, gift, costume, toy)
- Mega menu with dynamic product types + occasions
- Cake customizer (message on cake + delivery date/time picker)
- Add-on selector on product pages
- Suggested bundle (occasion kits)
- Planning Wizard (multi-step: occasion → budget → results)
- Sort by (price, newest)
- Basic SEO (sitemap, structured data, OG tags)
- Urgency banner (same-day delivery)
- Confetti/motion animations
- Account system (addresses, orders, profile)
- Mobile hamburger menu

## PHASE 1 — Trust & Conversion Essentials (High Impact, Quick Wins)

> Goal: Match table-stakes features every Indian e-commerce celebration site has.

| # | Feature | Benchmark Reference | Priority |

| 1.7 | **WhatsApp / Chat Support Widget** — Floating button for instant help | FNP, IGP | 🟡 High |
| 1.8 | **Order Tracking Page** — Post-purchase status with timeline | IGP (/searchorder), FNP | 🟡 High |

## PHASE 6 — Payments & Checkout Optimization

> Goal: Reduce cart abandonment, offer Indian payment preferences.

| # | Feature | Benchmark Reference | Priority |
|---|---------|-------------------|----------|
| 6.1 | **UPI / Razorpay / PhonePe Integration** — India-critical payment methods | IGP, FNP (all Indian gateways) | 🔴 Critical |
| 6.2 | **COD (Cash on Delivery)** — With nominal COD fee | IGP, FNP | 🔴 Critical |
| 6.3 | **EMI / Buy Now Pay Later** — Simpl, LazyPay, Klarna-equivalent | PartyDelights (Klarna/Clearpay) | 🟡 High |
| 6.4 | **Cart Abandonment Recovery** — Email/WhatsApp reminder after 1hr | Standard e-commerce | 🟡 High |
| 6.5 | **Guest Checkout** — Don't force registration | PartyCity, PartyDelights | 🟡 High |
| 6.6 | **Coupon / Promo Code System** — Visible deals page + header banner | IGP ("/offers-coupons-deals"), PartyCity | 🟡 High |
| 6.7 | **Express Checkout (1-click reorder)** — For returning customers | IGP | 🟢 Medium |
| 6.8 | **Split Payment** — Pay partially with wallet + card | Advanced | 🟢 Medium |
